Beach Restoration Project

Our flagship initiative focuses on restoring and protecting Sunset Beach through advanced erosion control, sand nourishment, and ecosystem rehabilitation. The project blends civil engineering with environmental stewardship, ensuring both resilience and community benefit.

Location: Sunset Beach, Coastal Zone
Status: In Progress
Current Phase: Sand Replenishment, Monitoring
Latest Progress:
  • Phase 1 is 75% complete (as of Feb 2025)
  • 45,000+ cubic yards of sand placed along northern section
  • Wave monitoring stations installed and operational
  • Environmental and water quality monitoring ongoing
